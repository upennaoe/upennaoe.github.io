Our Values

Alpha Omega Epsilon is a social and professional sorority with over 40 chapters throughout North America

Friendship

With social events such as date night, formal, Galentine's Day, and movie night, we develop a life-long commitment to our sisters with encouragement and support

Leadership

We provide opportunities where integrity, character and self-confidence can flourish, fostering enriched experiences and relationships

Professionalism

We promote future generations of women as engineers and scientists through professional events such as headshots and study sessions, and our alumni network
